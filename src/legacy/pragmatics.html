What is OPS

Why your data still can’t carry AI.

Most enterprises have data. Very few have data that AI can reliably reason from. The gap isn’t schema coverage or tooling — it’s pragmatic context: the judgement, constraints, and institutional knowledge that live in people’s heads, never in a schema. The Open Pragmatics Standard is the infrastructure to close that gap, permanently and in the open.

Read the whitepaper: Pragmatics & Enterprise AI →
Why semantic context breaks down at AI-scale
  • Context defined for humans gets misread by AI systems trained to generalise across all domains, not just yours.
  • Hand-crafted definitions rot as your business evolves — no agent updates them when the meaning shifts.
  • Coverage gaps compound: undocumented fields, tribal-knowledge edge cases, and contradictions between systems.
  • Global schemas assume a single truth that doesn’t exist across the silos, teams, and timelines of real enterprises.
How Pragmatics OS closes the gap
  • A living system of record that tracks what data means in use — not in theory.
  • Mirrors the institutional judgement of your best data experts, captured and made reproducible.
  • Open vocabulary ensures your context layer works with any AI system, any vendor, any stack.
  • Built to be maintained — benchmarks and tests surface drift before it becomes an AI reliability failure.
Resources

Everything you need to build in the open.

The Open Pragmatics Standard is designed to help all data practitioners build a performant data context layer that works with AI. It’s a toolkit that includes:

  • The standard — an open, vendor-neutral vocabulary for enterprise context: metrics, entities, policies, decisions, and the tribal knowledge that never made it into a schema.
  • Evaluation tools — open benchmarks and tests to measure how legible your data actually is to AI, and whether a change made it better.
  • Reference architectures — proven blueprints for standing up a pragmatic context layer on the stack you already run.
  • Playbooks — the patterns, step by step, for the work: migrations, metric harmonisation, keeping context current.
  • Community — of like-minded contributors working on the mission.

Published in the open, so your agents are never locked to anyone’s implementation — including ours.
