Pragmatics  /  03

The Semantic Layer is Dead.
Long live the semantic layer.

Your data stack was built for a world without AI. It makes sense that legacy frameworks, like semantics & ontologies, don’t work for AI-scale data work. Big Context is built on the back of the Pragmatic Layer and captures how your data is actually used in the real world.

Semantic Context
How you define your data

A hand-crafted vocabulary intended to be used across the org. Revenue means this. Customer means that.

Limited to what’s been recorded. Almost always stale.
Pragmatic Context
What your data means in actual use

A self-maintaining record that mirrors the judgement of your best data expert. Who relies on what table for which decisions. What caveats and nuances exist.

Dynamic, governed — ready for AI scale use.
