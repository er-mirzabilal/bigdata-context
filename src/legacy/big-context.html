Pragmatics  /  03

The semantic layer is dead. Long live the semantic layer.

The semantic layer is 35 years old, conceived for a pre-AI world. It’s a partial view of what your data means, built for static work.

AI requires a new paradigm. We call it the pragmatic layer — a 360° view of what your data means in context. We wrote the Open Pragmatics Standard (OPS) so you can have the playbooks, tools, reference architecture, and community to stand up a pragmatic context layer in house.
